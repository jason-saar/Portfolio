About Me

I'm a native Oregonian that's always been drawn towards technology and programming. When I was 10 years-old my mother bought me a book called The Hacker Diaries: Confessions of Teenage Hackers, and since that moment I had always dreamed of becoming a computer wiz. Fast-forward a year later, and I had begun teaching myself Python. Eventually my passion turned towards video-games for better or worse, and I began scripting bot scripts for a game called Runescape. In my teenage years I moved onto playing with assembly code and manipulating the memory of games. I've always been enthralled by coding and tinkering, and I bring that passion into every project I work on.
